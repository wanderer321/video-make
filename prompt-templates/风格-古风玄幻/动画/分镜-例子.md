少年盘腿坐在山顶，双手结印，月光洒在身上。
moonlight as key light, cool blue, soft fill from ground reflection.

Framing: medium shot
Move: locked-off static camera, no movement
Speed: N/A
Angle: eye level

cloth lags behind motion, catches up with overshoot.

灵气如涓涓细流在体内流转，皮肤发出微弱蓝光。
golden energy particles swirl slowly under the skin, catching the moonlight.

### 例子12: 冰火大战（哪吒vs敖丙四幕改编）

**来源**: [seedance-examples-zh #7](https://github.com/Emily2040/seedance-2.0/blob/main/skills/seedance-examples-zh/SKILL.md)
**类型**: Anime/Artistic | **难度**: Expert

```
【第一幕：静止与爆发】测试起步加速度
视觉：特写。修仙者的法器在手中震动，灵气溢出，火星四溅。
镜头突然急速拉远。
动作：修仙者突然蹬地跃起，地面碎裂。
Physics: dust erupts violently from the ground impact.

【第二幕：法术对撞】测试匹配剪辑转场
视觉：修仙者挥舞法剑横扫，划出一道金色剑气圆弧。
转场设计：剑气圆弧在旋转中无缝切换为魔修的暗黑能量波。

【第三幕：激烈法术交互】测试物理碰撞与镜头感
视觉：侧面中景。金色剑气与暗黑能量在半空中激烈碰撞。
动作：火花与暗影碎片同时爆裂。
魔修身形如鬼魅，绕着修仙者螺旋上升；
修仙者在空中翻身，脚踏飞剑俯冲。
Physics: sparks fly off in slow motion, dark fragments scatter.

【第四幕：终极转场】测试时空折叠
视觉：修仙者一剑击碎魔修的护盾。
转场设计：碎裂的能量片在空中幻化为飘落的花瓣。
```

## 📊 古风玄幻运镜预设表

| 运镜 | 官方术语 | 适用场景 | 提示词写法 |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|
| 锁定静止 | locked-off static camera | 修炼、对话 | `locked-off static camera, no movement` |
| 缓慢推近 | slow dolly push | 情绪递进 | `slow dolly push from medium to tight close-up over 8 seconds` |
| 缓慢拉远 | slow dolly pull back | 场景展示 | `slow dolly pull back revealing the full environment` |
| 环绕 | slow orbit | 角色展示 | `slow orbit left around the subject, constant distance` |
| 手持跟拍 | handheld tracking | 追逐 | `handheld tracking following the subject, subtle shake` |
| 升降 | crane shot | 大场面 | `crane shot rising from ground level to overhead` |
| 急速拉远 | fast dolly pull back | 战斗爆发 | `fast dolly pull back revealing the battlefield` |
| 一镜到底 | one continuous tracking | 追踪 | `one continuous tracking shot` |
| 缓慢横摇 | slow pan | 风景展示 | `slow pan left revealing [new element]` |
| 缓慢上摇 | slow tilt up | 威严展示 | `slow tilt up from [foreground] to [sky]` |
