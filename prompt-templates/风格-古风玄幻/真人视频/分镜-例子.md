## 📊 运镜预设表

| 运镜 | 官方术语 | 适用场景 | 提示词写法 |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|
| 环绕 | slow orbit | 角色展示 | `slow orbit left around the subject, constant distance` |
| 缓慢推近 | slow dolly push | 情绪递进 | `slow dolly push from medium to tight close-up over 8 seconds` |
| 锁定静止 | locked-off static | 对话 | `locked-off static camera, no movement` |
| 手持跟拍 | handheld tracking | 战斗 | `handheld tracking following the action, subtle shake` |
| 一镜到底 | one continuous tracking | 追踪 | `one continuous tracking shot` |
| 航拍拉远 | slow aerial pullback | 大场面 | `slow aerial pullback revealing the vast scale` |
