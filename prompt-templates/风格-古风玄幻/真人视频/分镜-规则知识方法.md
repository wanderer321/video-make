## 📐 核心原则

### Director's Formula: `[Subject] + [Action] + [Scene] + [Camera] + [Style] + [Constraints]`
**真人视频类型**: Drama/Narrative → 60-100字

### One-Move Rule
每镜头只选一个运镜。堆叠多个运镜是生成失败的最常见原因。

### 物理语言
- ❌ "beautiful magical energy" → ✅ "golden energy particles swirl slowly, catching the moonlight"
- ❌ "dramatic lighting" → ✅ `candle practical, warm gold, deep shadow beyond 1m`
