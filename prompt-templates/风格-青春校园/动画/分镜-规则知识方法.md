## 📐 核心原则（Seedance 2.0 官方）

### Director's Formula
```
[Subject 主体] + [Action 动作] + [Scene 场景] + [Camera 运镜] + [Style 风格] + [Constraints 约束]
```
**青春校园类型**: Lifestyle/Social → 40-60字 | Drama/Narrative → 60-100字

### One-Move Rule
**每个镜头只指定一个主要运镜**。

### Camera Contract
```
Framing: [wide / medium / close-up / tight close-up]
Move: [locked-off / dolly / pan / tilt / orbit / handheld / crane / tracking] (只选一个)
Speed: [slow / moderate / fast]
Angle: [eye level / low angle / high angle]
```

### 青春校园视觉语言
| 阶段 | 视觉方案 | 来源 |
|------|---------|------|
| 童年 | `warm natural sunlight, soft wrap shadows, handheld slight sway` | seedance-lighting |
| 青春期 | `golden hour light, subtle film grain, warm highlights cold shadows` | seedance-style |
| 成年重逢 | `warm golden hour light, nostalgic color grade, subtle film grain` | seedance-style |

### 标准运镜短语
- `locked-off static camera, no movement`
- `slow dolly push from medium shot to tight close-up over 8 seconds`
- `handheld, slight sway`
- `slow tracking alongside the subjects`
- `slow pan left revealing [new element]`
- `slow dolly pull back revealing the full environment`

### 风格锚点
- `subtle film grain, warm highlights cold shadows`
- `vintage softness, muted palette`
- `golden hour light, soft wrap shadows`
- `watercolor wash backgrounds, ink outline characters`

### 大气效果
- `dust motes suspended in the sunbeam from the window`
- `cloth lags behind motion, catches up with overshoot`
- `steam rising from the hot food`
- `breath misting in cold air at each exhale`
- `thin fog catching the moonlight, visibility 200m`

### 反 Slop 规则
- ❌ beautiful → 删除
- ❌ cinematic → 删除
- ✅ 用具体物理描述替代
