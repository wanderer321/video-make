### 例子1: 被活埋→爬出（逆袭开场）

**来源**: [seedance-examples-zh #1 豪门爽剧改编](https://github.com/Emily2040/seedance-2.0/blob/main/skills/seedance-examples-zh/SKILL.md)
**验证**: 墨雨云间 2024，吴谨言主演
**类型**: Drama/Action | **难度**: Expert | **时长**: 15秒

```
【风格】逆袭爽剧风格、高饱和度滤镜、极致面部特写、戏剧化情绪范围
【角色】被活埋的女主（满身泥泞，眼神从恐惧到冰冷坚定）

[00:00-00:05] Shot 1: 极致绝望。
雨夜，泥土中。一只手从土中伸出，手指沾满泥浆。
Framing: close-up on hand
Move: locked-off static camera
【动作】手开始挣扎，泥土松动。
mud dripping from fingers, heavy fabric lagging behind motion with overshoot.
light rain visible against the key light, not heavy.
【口型同步】（微弱）"沈……玉容……"

[00:05-00:10] Shot 2: 绝地反转。
薛芳菲从土中爬出来，满身泥泞，浑身颤抖。
Framing: medium shot
Move: slow dolly push from medium shot to tight close-up over 5 seconds
【动作】她缓缓站起，双腿颤抖但站稳。
雨水冲刷脸上的泥土，露出坚定的眼神。
sweat and rain mixing on her face.

[00:10-00:15] Shot 3: 复仇宣言。
极致面部特写。她的眼神从茫然变为冰冷。
Framing: tight close-up
Move: locked-off static camera
【口型同步】"那些人既然怕我活着，我就更应该活下去。"
高饱和度，戏剧化表情变化。
```

## 📊 逆袭爽剧运镜预设表

| 运镜 | 官方术语 | 适用场景 | 提示词写法 |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|
| 锁定静止 | locked-off static camera | 对峙、对话 | `locked-off static camera, no movement` |
| 缓慢推近 | slow dolly push | 气场变化 | `slow dolly push from medium to tight close-up over 8 seconds` |
| 缓慢拉远 | slow dolly pull back | 场景展示 | `slow dolly pull back revealing the full environment` |
| 环绕仰拍 | slow orbit + low angle | 战场重逢 | `slow orbit left around the subject, low angle` |
| 手持跟拍 | handheld tracking | 复仇之路 | `handheld tracking following the subject, subtle shake` |
| 低角度仰拍 | low angle | 权力反转 | `low angle, slow dolly push` |
| 一镜到底 | one continuous tracking | 追踪 | `one continuous tracking shot` |
| 缓慢横摇 | slow pan | 群像展示 | `slow pan left revealing [new element]` |
| 急速拉远 | fast dolly pull back | 战斗爆发 | `fast dolly pull back revealing the battlefield` |
| 升降 | crane shot | 大场面 | `crane shot rising from ground level to overhead` |
