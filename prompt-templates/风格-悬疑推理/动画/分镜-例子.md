## 📊 悬疑推理运镜预设表

| 运镜 | 官方术语 | 适用场景 | 提示词写法 |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|
| 手电筒跟拍 | handheld tracking | 案发现场 | `handheld tracking following the subject, subtle shake` |
| 缓慢推近面部 | slow dolly push | 表情变化 | `slow dolly push from medium to tight close-up over 8 seconds` |
| 静态中景 | locked-off static | 审讯对话 | `locked-off static camera, no movement` |
| 切反应镜头 | Cut to: [shot] | 对峙 | `[Cut to: Close-up, locked-off] 反应镜头` |
| 手持晃动 | handheld tracking | 紧张追逐 | `handheld tracking following the subject, subtle shake` |
| 俯拍证据 | high angle, locked | 线索发现 | `high angle, locked-off static camera` |
| 一镜到底 | one continuous tracking | 追踪 | `one continuous tracking shot` |
| 低角度仰拍 | low angle | 威严压迫 | `low angle, locked-off static camera` |
| 缓慢拉远 | slow dolly pull back | 场景展示 | `slow dolly pull back revealing the full environment` |
| 缓慢横摇 | slow pan | 群像展示 | `slow pan left revealing [new element]` |
