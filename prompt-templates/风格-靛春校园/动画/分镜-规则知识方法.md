## 📐 靛春校园动画分镜核心规则

### Director's Formula（导演公式）
```
[Subject 主体] + [Action 动作] + [Scene 场景] + [Camera 运镜] + [Style 风格] + [Constraints 约束]
```
**靛春校园类型**: Lifestyle/Social → 40-60字 | Drama/Narrative → 60-100字

### Camera Contract（靛春校园专用）
```
Framing: [wide / medium / close-up / tight close-up]
Move: [locked-off / slow dolly / slow pan / handheld slight sway / tracking]
Speed: [slow / moderate]
Angle: [eye level / slight low angle / slight high angle]
```

### One-Move Rule
**每个镜头只指定一个主要运镜**。靛春校园的运镜应以慢速、平稳为主。

### 靛春校园分镜色彩编码
| 场景类型 | 主色调 | 补色 | 光影关键词 |
|---------|-------|------|-----------|
| 办公/职场 | 靛蓝 (#2C3E6B) | 雾灰 (#8E9AAF) | cool natural daylight, window light, soft shadow |
| 咖啡馆/约会 | 暖橙 (#E8A87C) | 杏粉 (#F2C9D0) | warm ambient light, candle glow, golden hour |
| 深夜城中 | 夜蓝 (#1A1A2E) | 路灯黄 (#E8A87C) | street lamp, neon reflection, blue hour |
| 温馨家中 | 暖黄 (#F5E6CC) | 米白 (#F8F4E6) | soft warm light, morning sunlight, homey glow |
| 黄昏/记忆 | 紫蓝 (#4A3F6B) | 金橙 (#D4884A) | sunset silhouette, golden hour, nostalgic |

### 靛春校园常用运镜短语
- `locked-off static camera, no movement` — 对话/情感瞬间
- `slow dolly push from medium shot to close-up` — 心动的瞬间
- `slow tracking alongside the subjects` — 一起走路的日常
- `slow pan from subject to environment` — 从人切换到环境氛围
- `handheld, slight sway` — 刚认识时的紧张感/回忆片段
- `slow orbit around the subjects` — 情感高潮的关键瞬间
- `crane down from wide establishing shot to characters` — 场景引入

### 风格锚点
- `靛蓝 and warm orange color palette` — 色彩主调
- `soft wrap lighting, warm highlights cool shadows` — 光影特征
- `city nightscape background, bokeh light dots` — 都市夜景氛围
- `cinematic shallow depth of field` — 电影感浅景深
- `subtle film grain, muted palette` — 胶片质感

### 大气效果
- `steam rising from hot coffee in cold morning`
- `city lights reflecting on wet pavement after rain`
- `dust motes in sunset light from the window`
- `breath misting in cold air`
- `warm light spilling from convenience store onto dark street`

### 反 Slop 规则
- ❌ beautiful → 删除
- ❌ cinematic → 删除
- ✅ 用具体物理描述替代（靛蓝与暖橙的色彩对比、城市灯光的反射等）
