## 📐 核心原则（Seedance 2.0 官方）

### Director's Formula
```
[Subject 主体] + [Action 动作] + [Scene 场景] + [Camera 运镜] + [Style 风格] + [Constraints 约束]
```
**都市情感类型**: Drama/Narrative → 60-100字 | **生活社交**: Lifestyle → 40-60字

### One-Move Rule
**每个镜头只指定一个主要运镜**。堆叠多个运镜是生成失败的最常见原因。

### Camera Contract
```
Framing: [wide / medium / close-up / tight close-up / extreme close-up]
Move: [locked-off / dolly / pan / tilt / orbit / handheld / crane / tracking] (只选一个)
Speed: [slow / moderate / fast]
Angle: [eye level / low angle / high angle / dutch angle]
```

### 类型运镜预设
| 类型 | 推荐运镜 | 避免 |
|------|---------|------|
| 戏剧/叙事 | slow push-in, dolly pull-out, tracking, static | fast orbit, snap zoom |
| 生活/社交 | handheld, static, slow pan | crane, dolly zoom |
| 产品展示 | orbit, slow push-in, static | handheld, whip pan |
| 商业品牌 | tracking, crane up, slow push-in | shaky handheld |

### 标准运镜短语
- `locked-off static camera, no movement`
- `slow dolly push from medium shot to tight close-up over 8 seconds`
- `slow dolly pull back revealing the full environment`
- `slow pan left revealing [new element]`
- `handheld, subtle sway`
- `tracking alongside the cars, low to the ground`

### 灯光设计
- 暧昧期: `warm highlights cold shadows, subtle film grain`
- 商战博弈: `neon-saturated palette, anamorphic lens feel`
- 王家卫美学: `VHS scan lines, oversaturated color, warm highlights cold shadows`
- 高档餐厅: `neon sign through window as key light, cool blue, warm fill from table candle`
- ❌ dramatic lighting → ✅ `candle practical, warm gold, deep shadow beyond 1m`

### 大气效果
- `steam rising from hot dishes, catching the warm interior light`
- `dust motes suspended in the sunbeam from the window`
- `breath misting in cold air at each exhale`
- `cloth lags behind motion, catches up with overshoot`
- `glass reflections shift with camera movement`

### 风格锚点
- `anamorphic, subtle film grain, muted palette`
- `vintage softness, warm highlights cold shadows`
- `neon-saturated palette, warm highlights cold shadows`
- `VHS scan lines, oversaturated color`

### 反 Slop 规则
- ❌ cinematic → 删除（默认就是cinematic）
- ❌ beautiful → 删除
- ❌ dramatic → 删除
- ✅ 用具体物理描述替代
