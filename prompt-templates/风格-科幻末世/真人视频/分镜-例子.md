## 📊 运镜预设表

| 运镜 | 官方术语 | 适用场景 | 提示词写法 |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|
| 航拍拉远 | slow aerial pullback | 大场面 | `slow aerial pullback revealing the vast scale` |
| 宏观到微观 | slow dolly push | 科学发现 | `slow dolly push from macro to tight close-up` |
| 手持跟拍 | handheld tracking | 紧张探索 | `handheld tracking following the subject, subtle shake` |
| 环绕仰拍 | slow orbit + low angle | 巨型物体 | `slow orbit left around the subject, low angle` |
| 主观视角 | close-up (POV) | 幻觉/异常 | `close-up (POV), locked-off static camera` |
| 一镜到底 | one continuous tracking | 追踪 | `one continuous tracking shot` |
| 缓慢横摇 | slow pan | 废墟展示 | `slow pan left revealing [new element]` |
| 锁定静止 | locked-off static | 对话 | `locked-off static camera, no movement` |
